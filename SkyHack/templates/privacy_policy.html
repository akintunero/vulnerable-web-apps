Compliance Notice

SkyHack Airlines is committed to protecting your privacy and is compliant with:

  • NDPR - Nigerian Data Protection Regulation
  • GDPR - General Data Protection Regulation
  • PCI DSS - Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard
  • ISO 27001 - Information Security Management

2. Personal Data We Collect

Flight Booking Information:
  • Passenger names and contact details
  • Passport information and travel documents
  • Flight preferences and seat selections
  • Payment information (securely processed)
Account Information:
  • Email address and password
  • Frequent flyer program details
  • Travel history and preferences
  • Communication preferences

3. Legal Basis for Processing (NDPR Article 2.4)

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Contract Performance: To fulfill flight bookings and provide services
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve our services and ensure security
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with aviation and immigration laws
  • Consent: For marketing communications (where applicable)

4. Data Retention (NDPR Article 3.1)

We retain your personal data for the following periods:

  • Booking Information: 7 years (regulatory requirement)
  • Account Data: Until account deletion or 3 years of inactivity
  • Payment Information: As required by PCI DSS standards
  • Security Logs: 2 years for security monitoring

5. Your Rights (NDPR Article 3.1)

Under NDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Information: Know what data we collect and how we use it
  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for marketing communications

6. Data Security (NDPR Article 3.1)

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • PCI DSS compliant payment processing
  • ISO 27001 certified information security management
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Data backup and disaster recovery procedures

7. Data Sharing and Transfers

We may share your data with:

  • Government Authorities: Immigration, customs, and security agencies
  • Service Providers: Payment processors, IT services, and logistics partners
  • Other Airlines: For code-share and interline agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or court order

Note: All data transfers comply with NDPR requirements for cross-border data transfers.
