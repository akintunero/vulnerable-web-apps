Frequently Asked Questions

How to Vote

1. Register for an Account

Create your account using a valid email address and secure password. You'll receive a confirmation email.

2. Log In to Your Account

Use your email and password to access your voting dashboard.

3. Review Candidates

Read about each candidate's background, policies, and vision before making your decision.

4. Cast Your Vote

Select your preferred candidate and confirm your vote. You can only vote once per election.

5. View Results

Check the results page to see live vote tallies and election outcomes.

Common Questions

Can I change my vote after submitting?

No, votes cannot be changed once submitted. Please review your choice carefully before confirming.

How do I know my vote was counted?

You'll receive a confirmation message after voting. You can also check the results page to see updated vote counts.

What if I forget my password?

Contact our support team at support@evotnow.com for password recovery assistance.

Is my vote anonymous?

Yes, individual votes are encrypted and anonymous. Only aggregate results are publicly displayed.

When does the election end?

The current election ends in 7 days. Check the dashboard for the exact countdown timer.

Can I vote from my mobile device?

Yes, our platform is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets.
